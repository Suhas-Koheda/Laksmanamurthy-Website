Ramaka Lakshmanamurthy





Dr.Ramaka Lakshmanamurthy was born to shree Ramaka Gopalaramiah and Tulasamma on 1st-June-1938 at Husnabad(former Karimnagar District) . When he was 3 years old he lost his mother. At that time, he was brought to Indurthi by his maternal uncles Shree Kundojjala Venkata Narsaiah and Shree Kundojjala Ramachandraiah.

During his childhood he was brought up by his uncles.He was educated in Indurti village school in Urdu medium till 5th standard , after that he studied Sanskrit optionally till 10th class in Karimnagar Government Multi Purpose School and was passed in first class. He studied in Intermediate Arts and Science College in Warangal and was passed in First division. He initially studied BSC (ZOOLOGY) in Nizam College in Hyderabad and with the help of his friends and his uncles , he completed his MBBS from Osmania Medical College in 1963 and MD in 1968. He served for the government in between 1964 and 1984 , after that he retired and started his private practise and he spent his life in serving patients.

As he belonged to a traditional family, he performed Puja and Rudrabhishekam daily. He made "Amrutha Pashupata Rudrabhishekam" popular. With his passion in Literature, he studied many Spiritual texts.He began his writing with a book "Sri Kiranalu"-a book with devotional Mangala Haarati and in his last days of life he wrote "Sri Charanalu"-a book with devotional songs. He wrote a collection of Essays called"Samarcha".

After going through many books of "Hanuman Chalisa by Tulsidas" , he wrote a Commentary, Gradually it was translated to Hindi and English languages. For his Hindi Commentary, he was awarded a prize of 1 lakh Rupees and a Certificate of Excellence by Kendirya Hindi Vidyalaya.

He excelled not only in Literature but also in Music. During his Middle age he started learning music . He learnt music in a way that could satisfy all the music lovers and sung "Hanuman Chalisa" in 40 Verses and gave it to Hanuman devotees in form of CD.

In 1997 , he was felicitated by 8 Vice- Chancellors at NIT Warangal (National Institute of Technology-Warangal) , which was attended by many people from Northern Telangana. In that felicitation ceremony, a book titled "Sri Chandanamu"-which was written by people who gained utmost importance in their respective profession, was unveiled. He was also considered as 'Ambassador of Peace' on World Peace Day.

Professioanlly and personnaly he gained intimacy with some of the great personalities. ---He once got a chance to treat Sri Sri Sri Tridandi Pedda Jeeyar Swamy. --- He was blessed by Sri Ganapati Sacchidanada Swamyji for the welcome speech by him and was given a Japamala. ---He received blessings by Sri Kurtalla Swamy. ---He received blessings by Sri Sundara Chaitanya Swamy. ---With his intimacy with Sri Shivanada Swamy , he received blessings from Sri Shivanada Swamy. Dring his life he also a close person with Biruduraju Ramaraju garu, Professor Jayashankar garu, Prof. Jagannath garu ,Sri Kaloji Narayana Rao garu and Sri Hayagriva Chari garu and Sri Mandadi Narasimhareddy garu.
